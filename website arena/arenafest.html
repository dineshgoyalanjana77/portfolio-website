Arena Fest

About Arena Fest

Arena Fest is a one-day cultural extravaganza that brings together Arena students for a day of exciting competitions and healthy rivalry giving them a stage to showcase their talents beyond the Media and Entertainment sector. The event features an Exclusive Talent Show, where students can showcase their unique skills, and an intense Dance War, where participants battle it out with energy and style. The Open Mic Competitions give performers—singers, rappers, and poets—a stage to share their creativity, while the Fashion Show allows students to express their fashion sense and design ideas. In the Cosplay segment, participants dress as their favorite characters, adding a fun and imaginative twist. The event culminates with a high-energy DJ Night, offering a lively finale with electrifying beats and a vibrant atmosphere.
