3D VFX

Visual Effects (VFX)
is the process of creating, altering, or enhancing imagery for films and other media that does not capture live-action footage. VFX integrates live-action footage with computer-generated imagery (CGI) to create realistic environments or simulate fantastical elements that would be dangerous or impractical to film in real life.VFX plays a crucial role in modern filmmaking, allowing filmmakers to push the boundaries of storytelling and create captivating visual experiences for audiences.
Game Design
Doing Game Design means inventing, defining, and describing every element of a Game Experience and its interactions with other elements. Every bit of what you see, hear, and feel when playing a game is a designer's decision to achieve a specific experience. Game Design lives inside the broader topic of Game Development, which groups together all the roles needed to create a game (design, programming, art, sound, testing, etc.).

Designing (Graphics & Web)

Graphic design
is a rapidly growing industry in India that encompasses creativity, technology, and communication. It plays a vital role in branding, advertising, and marketing strategies of businesses. With the increasing digitization and visual-driven content in India, graphic design has become indispensable.
UI/UX design
is critical for websites and digital products as it directly impacts user interaction, engagement, and satisfaction. While UI (User Interface) focuses on the visual and interactive elements, UX (User Experience) focuses on the overall experience and usability of the product.UI Design refers to the interface through which users interact with a website or digital product. Its primary goal is to capture attention and provide an aesthetically pleasing experience.
