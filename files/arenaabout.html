Our Story
Welcome to Arena Animation

As one of the best animation institutes in Indore, Arena Animation offers highly demanding professional courses where creativity comes to life. We are committed to bridging the gap between education and industry.

Located at Apollo Trade Center, Indore, we provide world-class training in Animation, VFX, Game Design, Graphic Design, and Web Development — all under one roof with expert faculty and modern infrastructure.

Our students have gone on to work at leading studios, advertising agencies, and tech companies across India, making Arena Animation a trusted name in creative education.
